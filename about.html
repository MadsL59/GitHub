My Story

I am a driven and self-motivated UI/UX Designer based in London, with a great sense of taste and eye for details, eager to take ideas from concept to wireframe, prototype to production. I have experience in understanding clients and delivering visual content for print and online for a 614k weekly audience as well as achieving sales targets.

My goal is to produce exciting and user-centric solutions in order to provide support for people that are interested in travel, self-care, well-being, fashion or fintech.My life mantra is ALWAYS love what you do and NEVER settle for less. In addition to my work and passion for UI Design, I am also a photographer, love travelling and always eager to learn new things.
